Double Chocolate Mug Cake

Ingredients Needed:

  • 1/4 cup of flour
  • 1/4 cup of sugar
  • 2 tablespoons of cocoa powder
  • pinch of salt
  • 1/4 cup of milk
  • 2 tablespoons of oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon chocolate chips
