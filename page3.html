Double Chocolate Mug Cake

Recipe Steps:

  1. Add flour, cocoa, sugar, baking powder and salt in to mug

  2. Mix all dry ingredients together

  3. Add vanilla extract

  4. Add milk and oil

  5. Stir everything together until all lumps disappear

  6. Add chocolate chips

  7. Gently fold in chocolate chips

  8. Place mug into the microwave

  9. Set the microwave for high and bake for 2 minutes

  10. Let cool for a bit and then add whip cream if desired
