Double Chocolate Mug Cake

Do you like chocolate? Are you craving something sweet? This quick and easy recipe is a perfect solution. It is an invidual serving of chocolate cake, instead of having to make a whole cake. This recipe does not require eggs, but it tastes the same as any other cake. I personally love to use almond milk instead of cow's milk, which would make this entirely vegan. Feel free to add more chocolate chips if you want a more molten lava cake effect.
